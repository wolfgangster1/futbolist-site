The Idea

Every year, the game lets good players go.

Contracts end. Phones go quiet. Careers that should still be alive just stop. The Futbolist is where they keep going. One team. Free agents who refuse to disappear, training every day, filming every step, chasing the contract that brings them back.

We don't sell the result.
We show the fight.
